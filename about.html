About Gamra Wood Craft

Heritage Moroccan woodworking shaped with modern design.

Our Story

Gamra Wood Craft was founded with a simple vision: to create exceptional wooden furniture that combines traditional Moroccan craftsmanship with contemporary design. What started as a small workshop in the heart of Gamra has grown into a respected name in custom furniture.

For over a decade, we've been dedicated to perfecting our craft, working with premium materials, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Every piece we create tells a story of passion, skill, and commitment to excellence.

Our artisans carry forward techniques passed down through generations, infusing each piece with authentic character and uncompromising quality.
